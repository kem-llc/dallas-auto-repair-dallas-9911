About Dallas Auto Repair

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep Dallas drivers safe and satisfied.

Dallas Auto Repair has been serving the Dallas community for over 20 years. Founded by master mechanic John D., our shop started as a small two-bay garage and has grown into a trusted full-service repair facility. We've built our reputation on integrity, quality workmanship, and customer care.

Today, our team of ASE-certified technicians continues that tradition. We invest in the latest diagnostic equipment and ongoing training to ensure we can handle any repair, from routine maintenance to complex engine overhauls. We treat every car as if it were our own.
