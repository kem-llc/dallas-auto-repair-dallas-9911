Brake Repair & Replacement

Restore stopping power with professional brake inspections, pad replacement, and rotor resurfacing.

Overview

Your brakes are your vehicle's most critical safety system. We inspect pads, rotors, calipers, and brake lines to identify wear. Our service includes high-quality pads and resurfacing or replacement of rotors to ensure smooth, quiet stopping.

What's included

  • Enhanced safety
  • Quieter braking
  • Longer brake life
  • Free brake inspection

How it works

Common questions

How do I know if my brakes need service?

Common signs include squealing, grinding, vibration, or a soft brake pedal. If you notice any of these, bring your car in for an inspection.

How long does a brake job take?

Most brake repairs are completed within 2-3 hours, depending on the extent of work needed.
